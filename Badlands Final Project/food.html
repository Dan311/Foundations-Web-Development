Bob's Breakfast Diner

Diner with a good old-fashioned breakfast!

In the mood for some good, homegrown cooking? If so, then Bob's is your place. Mosey on in! You have the choice of bellying up to the counter or sitting in a cozy booth to admire the scenery of the Black Hills. The food is the real hit here. Try the pancake sandwich and you'll be glad you did. Other hits are the Oatmeal Bonfire (don't ask) and the Belgian Waffle Surprise! Both are favorites and have tourists coming back for more. Come early! Any time after 8:00 and you'll be competing with every tourist in the Black Hills.

  • Pancakes big enough to feed your whole family!

Bluto's Burgers and Shakes

Good Greasy Spoon for the budget minded vacationer

Bluto makes a good burger and is not afraid to admit it. A no-frills style is made up for with an attention to detail. A lot of hits here but one miss is the Popeye burger - who wants cold, canned spinach on a burger? Not Bluto. Shakes are out of this world. All are made with a custard-base and mixed with an unrivaled quality. The kids will love the video games in the back. Just bring enough quarters to beat them in Pole Position.

  • Try a mouthwatering burger with a thirst-quenching shake!

Ramen Bowl

Cheap eats and great ramen!

The 'Bowl' is your go to spot for a delicious, hearty bowl of authentic ramen. Broth to die for and a soft-boiled egg in each one - this place has it all. If you're not the spicy type they are more than willing to accomodate. A solid fixture in Custer for the adventurous - be sure to ask for the chef's special menu. Wholesome and fresh ingredients a mile longYou'll be glad you did. Authentic food like this used to be hard to find in these here parts, but not anymore.

  • Bowl of simmering ramen

Rushmore Pizza

A pizza for every President!

We Americans like our pizza almost as much as we like our presidents. At Rushmore Pizza there is a pie for all our country's past presidents. Feeling hungry for four pizza's? The Mt. Rushmore is 4 of your favorites stacked on top of each other. Eat it all in one sitting with no reversal of fortune and you won't have to pay!

  • Presidential Pizza!

Breakfast Machine

A Healthy Breakfast!

For all those who are practically competitive vacationers, the folks at Breakfast Machine will have your chasis oiled and full of breakfast in no time. Up a bit late riling it up with the locals? The "Machine" will offer their 'black oil coffee" when you step in. They have all the standards and much of the menu is very affordable. Try the cinnamon roll and you'll never stop going back for more.

  • Healthy Breakfast to get you going!
